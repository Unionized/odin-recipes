The Stakes of Steak

Description

This sirloin steak recipe is served with very garlicky butter that makes this steak melt-in-your-mouth wonderful! I have never tasted any other steak that came even close to this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 pounds beef top sirloin steaks
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Stir in garlic and garlic powder, then set aside
  3. Season the steaks with salt and pepper
  4. Cook on a preheated grill for 3-4 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 125F
  5. Transfer the steaks to a plate and brush with the garlic butter infusion. Allow the steak to rest before eating
